Alex Kidd Tribute is a recreation of the classic Alex Kidd in Miracle World developed as a university project at UPC. The goal of the project was to reproduce the original gameplay experience while implementing core game systems, user interfaces, level design, art assets and additional mechanics.

In addition to recreating the original experience, the team implemented a fully functional Pogo Stick mechanic, allowing players to jump higher by holding the jump button and defeat enemies by landing on them while using the Pogo Stick.
